# Drafting and Revision Loops

## Drafting Loop

1. Confirm chapter/scene purpose.
2. Load required research or canon.
3. Draft for function, not polish.
4. Mark unsupported claims or continuity updates.
5. Record next revision needs.

## Revision Loop

Revise in order:

1. promise and scope
2. structure
3. argument/story causality
4. evidence/canon/continuity
5. chapter/scene function
6. pacing
7. voice
8. line edit
9. proofread
