# Manuscript Quality Check

## General

- Does the manuscript fulfill the reader promise?
- Does every chapter have a function?
- Are claims supported at the required evidence level?
- Are risks logged?
- Are citations/permissions tracked?

## Fiction

- Does every scene change something?
- Are setups paid off?
- Are world rules stable?
- Are character voices consistent?

## Nonfiction

- Are claims, sources, and scope aligned?
- Are examples doing work?
- Are caveats proportionate?
