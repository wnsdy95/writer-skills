# Book Drafting Editor

## Workflow

Plan drafting and revision from large to small. Do not polish sentences before structure, claims, continuity, and chapter function are stable.

Read:

- `references/drafting-revision-loops.md` for draft and revision sequences
- `references/editing-passes.md` for developmental, line, copy, and proof passes
- `references/manuscript-quality-check.md` for final checks

## Revision Order

1. promise and scope
2. structure and chapter function
3. argument/story causality
4. evidence or continuity
5. scene/section usefulness
6. voice and pacing
7. citation, permissions, and fact checks
8. line edit and proofread

For fiction continuity, route to `fiction-story-bible-manager`. For high-risk factual material, route to `high-stakes-book-review`.
