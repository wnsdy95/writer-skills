# Risk Routing

## Escalate to High Stakes Review

Escalate when the material includes:

- medical, health, legal, financial, or investment advice
- allegations about real people or organizations
- true crime, abuse, trauma, political/social conflict
- private or identifying information
- vulnerable groups
- sensitive history
- safety/security instructions
- copyrighted material, lyrics, long quotes, or image rights

## Risk Levels

- Low: invented or low-stakes creative material.
- Moderate: factual claims or named institutions.
- High: health, law, finance, real people, private facts.
- Severe: direct advice or allegations likely to cause harm.

Output a `Risk Register` when risk is high or severe.
