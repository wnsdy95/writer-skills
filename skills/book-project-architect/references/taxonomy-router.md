# Taxonomy Router

## Writer-First Types

- Fiction: invented narrative, genre expectation, theme, emotional truth.
- Narrative nonfiction: real events or people shaped as story.
- Practical nonfiction: reader transformation and repeatable application.
- Scholarly/technical: concepts, argument, evidence, technical precision.
- Visual/hybrid: image, page, spread, caption, or sequence is structural.

## Routing Signals

- "소설", "인물", "플롯", "장면": fiction.
- "타임라인", "떡밥", "회수", "설정 기억": fiction story bible.
- "회고록", "실존", "전기", "역사", "르포": narrative nonfiction.
- "방법", "프레임워크", "체크리스트", "실용": practical nonfiction.
- "기술서", "학술", "연구", "논지", "코드": scholarly/technical.
- "그림책", "만화", "사진", "요리", "여행", "도감": visual/hybrid.
- "의학", "법률", "금융", "실명", "범죄": high-stakes review.

## Korean Publishing Layer

If the book targets Korean readers, Korean language, or Korean publication, add:

- KDC/DDC or institutional subject position when useful
- ISBN/Korean book number and add-on-code planning when near publication
- ONIX-style metadata packet
- Korean bookstore category paths
- web novel/platform tags for serial genre fiction
- Korean spelling, spacing, punctuation, loanword, honorific, and dialect policy

Do not treat library classification, ISBN content code, and bookstore category as the same decision. Use all three when the book is moving toward publication.
