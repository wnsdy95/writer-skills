# Narrative Nonfiction Writing

## Workflow

Build narrative momentum without losing factual accountability. Separate documented fact, interview memory, author inference, reconstruction, and scene craft.

Read:

- `references/narrative-nonfiction-playbook.md` for structure
- `references/evidence-map.md` for fact-to-source mapping
- `references/interview-archive-plan.md` for interviews and archives

Route to `high-stakes-book-review` for true crime, allegations, named people, private facts, politics, vulnerable groups, or sensitive history.
