# High Stakes Book Review

## Workflow

Review for harm, not just correctness. This skill does not replace legal, medical, financial, or other professional review. It identifies claims, evidence thresholds, ethical risks, and revision requirements.

Read:

- `references/risk-model.md` for risk domains
- `references/evidence-thresholds.md` for minimum support
- `references/expert-review-checklist.md` for escalation

## Risk Triggers

Use this skill when material includes:

- medical or health advice
- legal or financial advice
- allegations about real people or organizations
- true crime or active disputes
- private facts or identifying details
- vulnerable groups
- political/social conflict
- sensitive history
- copyrighted material or extensive quotation
