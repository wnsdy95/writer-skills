# Fact Checking

## Claim Check Steps

1. Extract exact claims from the proposed chapter or outline.
2. Classify each claim by risk and evidence burden.
3. Attach a source or mark as unsupported.
4. Check source currency, authority, independence, and corroboration.
5. Rewrite, soften, qualify, or remove unsupported claims.
