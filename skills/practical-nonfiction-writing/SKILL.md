# Practical Nonfiction Writing

## Workflow

Design around reader transformation. Define the reader's problem, promise a scoped result, then build a usable method with examples, exercises, checklists, and troubleshooting.

Read:

- `references/practical-nonfiction-playbook.md` for book patterns
- `references/framework-design.md` for methods and models
- `references/exercise-checklist-plan.md` for application design

Escalate to `high-stakes-book-review` for health, legal, financial, safety, or professional claims.
